State channel

A DADS layer 2 solution to process the micropayment by every ad impression.

Real-time Bidding (RTB)

RTB utilizes computer algorithms to automatically buy and sell ads in real-time.

Decentralized Storage

IPFS will be used to keep advertisement-related media, such as images, videos and larger media (e.g video/interactive ads), etc

We are working towards a multi-chain web3 ad platform for advertisers, publishers and users with any chain and fully decentralized network that hosts privacy data, contents and more.

OUR PROJECT

DADS Ad Platform is a decentralized advertising platform to change user habits of receiving ads and increase interaction between advertisiers, publishers, users through blockchain technology. Where user-data is protected and decentralized, advertisers and publishers can well interact to their users. DADS Ad Platform is effective advertising solution for web3 and metaverse DApps.
